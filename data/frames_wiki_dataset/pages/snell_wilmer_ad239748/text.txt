Snell & Wilmer is an American law firm based in Phoenix, Arizona. Founded in 1938, it focuses on business law and has offices in 17 locations throughout the United States and in Mexico, including Los Angeles, Orange County, Palo Alto, and San Diego, California; Phoenix and Tucson, Arizona; Dallas, Texas; Denver, Colorado; Washington, D.C.; Boise, Idaho; Las Vegas and Reno-Tahoe, Nevada; Albuquerque, New Mexico; Portland, Oregon; Salt Lake City, Utah; Seattle, Washington; and Los Cabos, Mexico. The firm represents clients ranging from large, publicly traded corporations, to small businesses, individuals and entrepreneurs. Barbara J. Dawson is the chair.
