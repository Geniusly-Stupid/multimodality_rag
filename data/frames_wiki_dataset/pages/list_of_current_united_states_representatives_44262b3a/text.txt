This is a list of individuals serving in the United States House of Representatives (as of November 12, 2025, the 119th Congress). The membership of the House comprises 435 seats for representatives from the 50 states, apportioned by population, as well as six seats for non-voting delegates from U.S. territories and the District of Columbia. As of November 12, 2025, there are 433 representatives and 2 vacancies.

Partisan affiliation by state
As of November 12, 2025, Republicans control 30 state delegations and Democrats control 18 state delegations. Two state delegations (Colorado and Minnesota) are evenly split.
